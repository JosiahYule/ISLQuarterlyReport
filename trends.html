How Projections Work

  • Pace projection (amber) — Uses the formula P = (V / Delapsed) × Dtotal, where V is the cumulative total to date, Delapsed is the number of calendar days since the quarter started, and Dtotal is the total calendar days in the quarter (92 for Q3 Mar–May). This yields the observed daily rate, then extrapolates it across the full quarter. For example: if 19 days have elapsed and 13,000 impressions are recorded, the daily rate is 13,000 ÷ 19 ≈ 684/day, and the projection is 684 × 92 ≈ 62,960. Each card also shows the Q2 daily rate (Q2 total ÷ 90 days) so you can directly compare velocity, independent of where the projected total lands.
  • Followers — A snapshot metric (total at quarter end), not a cumulative count. No pace projection is shown; the card displays Q1 and Q2 actuals for reference.
  • Data is fetched live from Hootsuite via Google Apps Script and auto-refreshes every 5 minutes.
